Chapel Script is a classic, high-quality script font. 
It is carefully hand-drawn by Måns Grebäck with inspiration from 1900's show card and logotype design. 

With its more than 700 original glyphs, the typeface supports almost all Latin languages. 
Chapel Script contains contextual and stylistic alternates to give each word a customized and hand-crafted impression.
The ligatures makes each letter connect smoothly. The initial, final and swash alternates ensures soft and natural ending to each word.

Also included is a bonus font with alternate letters, which contains ten swashes to give your logo an extra effect.

Use this font for logotypes, headlines and slogans. 
With it's timeless class, it will make your graphic project fresh but reliable.
